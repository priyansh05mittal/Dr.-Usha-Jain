About Dr. Usha Jain


Dr. Usha Jain earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Central University of Rajasthan, focusing on secure authentication mechanisms in Wireless Sensor Networks. With a strong academic foundation and industrial exposure, she has served as an Assistant Professor at leading institutions like Manipal University Jaipur and Amity University Rajasthan.
